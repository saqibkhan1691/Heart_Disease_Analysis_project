About Heart Disease

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide and remains a critical public health concern. It encompasses a range of cardiovascular conditions influenced by age, lifestyle habits, genetic predisposition, and underlying medical disorders. Early identification of risk factors plays a vital role in reducing long-term complications and improving overall health outcomes.

This project focuses on analyzing structured health data to uncover meaningful patterns associated with heart disease risk. By examining demographic attributes such as age and gender, lifestyle indicators including smoking and physical activity, and medical conditions like diabetes and obesity, we aim to understand how different variables contribute to disease prevalence.

The objective of this analysis is not merely visualization but insight generation. Through interactive dashboards and structured storytelling, the platform highlights key risk drivers and supports data-informed decision-making. By transforming raw health data into actionable insights, this project demonstrates how analytics can contribute to preventive healthcare strategies.
